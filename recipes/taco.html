The El Taco Recipe

This is our most favorite dish in our old menu. The infamous El Taco EST. 1952

Why the Dish?

Our taco was founded in 1952 in Mexico (Rancho - Neuvo Centro) by my great grandmother Maria Martinez She had a passion for food after my great grandfather would take her out to eat at other resturants near their ranch, while eating some carne she decied to try it with some tortillas, silandro, onion and some lime with green salsa. She then realized that putting all of this combines toghther was really good, so that day she decided to start preping that type of food for my g grandfather before he would leave to farm for the day. She then extended it to all my family and they soon began to enjoy it. Her daughter (my grandmother) thought it would be a good idea to start selling them infront of their house as it was located across the dirt path from the local elementary school. And so eventually it become really famous and so my g grandfather started helping as well with preping and cooking the meat. They would sell out everyday during lunch hour as more people started comming. So my g grandfather wanted to open his dream store a Marketa con comida . It became so succsessfull they eventaully bought a home in the city and reopened the store there and sold even more.
